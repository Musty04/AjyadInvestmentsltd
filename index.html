About Ajyad Investment

Ajyad Investment Limited is a diversified investment company with a strong presence in various sectors, including oil and gas, mining, real estate, agriculture and agro-allied, education and training, and consultancy.

Company Profile

Our Vision

To be a leading investment company in Africa, driven by innovation, strategic focus, and commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

To provide our stakeholders with sustainable returns on investment by leveraging our expertise and network in identifying profitable opportunities in our areas of focus.

Our Core Values

- Integrity: We operate with transparency and integrity in all our dealings with all stakeholders.

- Innovation: We continuously seek new and innovative ways to drive growth and return on investment.

- Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, ensuring high-quality services and products.

Our Investment Sectors

Oil & Gas

Strategic investments in upstream and downstream operations with focus on sustainable energy solutions and infrastructure development.

Mining

Mineral exploration, extraction projects, and trading operations across valuable resource sectors including gold, tin, and industrial minerals.

Real Estate

Development and investment in residential, commercial, and industrial properties in prime locations across Nigeria's major cities.

Agriculture

Agricultural production, processing facilities, and bulk marketing operations focusing on food security and export commodities.

Education

Training and education services for individuals and organizations, focusing on vocational skills and professional development programs.

Consultancy

Strategic financial and technical advisory services for businesses and government entities across our sectors of expertise.
